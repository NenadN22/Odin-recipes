Waldorf Salad

Description

A Waldorf salad is a fruit and nut salad that features a mayonnaise dressing. It was created in 1896 by Oscar Tschirky, the maître d'hôtel at New York’s.

Waldorf-Astoria. It doesn’t traditionally contain meat, but chicken is often added to make it a complete meal.

Ingredients

These are the simple ingredients you’ll need to make this restaurant-worthy Waldorf salad recipe at home:

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 3 apples — peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1 cup thinly sliced celery
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts
  • ½ cup raisins (Optional)
