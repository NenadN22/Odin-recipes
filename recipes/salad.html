Waldorf Salad

Description

A Waldorf salad is a fruit and nut salad that features a mayonnaise dressing. It was created in 1896 by Oscar Tschirky, the maître d'hôtel at New York’s.

Waldorf-Astoria. It doesn’t traditionally contain meat, but chicken is often added to make it a complete meal.
